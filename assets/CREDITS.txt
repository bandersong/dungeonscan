EVERYTHING ELSE
---------------
The parchment textures, stains, coffee rings, compass roses, decorative
borders, scale bars and title cartouches are all drawn by MapSmith itself
in code, so they stay crisp at any print size and carry no extra licenses.

Thank you to all of these artists and type designers. 💛
